About Me

Mubarak Abubakar Bako (born April 1, 1995), known professionally as Peemkay, is a Nigerian hip hop recording artist, songwriter, record producer, and software engineer.

As the president and co-founder of North Central Boys Entertainment and NCB Records, I've been pushing the boundaries of Nigerian hip hop while simultaneously pursuing my passion for technology.

With 2 studio albums and three mixtapes under my belt, I've established myself as a versatile artist who produces much of my own material. My unique perspective as both a musician and software engineer allows me to bridge the gap between art and technology.
